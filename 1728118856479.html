What is Disability?

Disability can be defined as a physical or mental condition that limits a person's movements, senses, or activities. It encompasses a wide range of impairments, including mobility, cognitive, sensory, and mental health issues.

Importance of Awareness

Raising awareness about disabilities helps foster understanding and inclusion, reducing stigma and discrimination. It is essential for creating supportive environments where everyone can thrive.
